Rock , Paper, Scissors

Instructions

You (player) are to make a pick from the pictures below either : Rock, Paper, or Scissors. Your pick will be matched against the CPU player and a winner will be determined. The rules are as follows:

  • -Paper beats Rock
  • -Rock beats Scissors
  • -Scissors beats Paper
  • -Draw if both players select same option
